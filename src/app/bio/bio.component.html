Biography

it all started when i was born... \n I grew up in the suburbs of Orlando Florida, where the mouse is king. I found my way into the sport of swimming and water polo during high school. I continued to play club water polo through college while i got my BS in Mechanical Engineering from UCF. After graduating i got a job as a design engineer for a magnetics company on the east coast of florida. I enjoyed creating new magnetic coils and working with the prototype team to craft the component to the customers specification. After i had my fill of the beach i wanted to fulfill a dream of moving to Colorado and snowboard as much as possible. Then i discovered programming and decided to enroll in the Flatiron School immersive web development bootcamp. I loved the experience and want nothing more than to work with friends in an exciting evironment while i code the future of the internet. Soon after graduating i got a job at IBM in Baton Rouge, LA where i work to this day as a consultant to large companies as they adopt new technologies.
